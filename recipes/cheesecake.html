Cheesecake Dish

This cheesecake is perfect for any dinner date for two.

Ingredients

  • Sunflower Oil
  • Digestive Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • White Chocolate
  • Strawberries
  • Cream Cheese
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Lemon
  • Maple Syrup
  • Double Cream

Steps

  1. Make the biscuits.
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Layer the ingredients.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the cheesecake rest before serving.
